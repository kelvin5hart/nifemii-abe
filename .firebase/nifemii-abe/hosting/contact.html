Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom outfit take to make?

Custom outfits typically take 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity of the design. For wedding attire, we recommend booking at least 6-8 weeks in advance to allow for multiple fittings and adjustments.

What is your payment process?

We require a 50% deposit to begin work on your custom piece. The remaining balance is due before final delivery. We accept bank transfers and cash payments.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International clients can have virtual consultations via video call, and we&apos;ll guide you through the measurement process. Shipping costs vary by destination.

Can I provide my own fabric?

Absolutely! You can bring your own fabric, or we can help source premium materials for you. We have access to a wide range of fabrics including aso-oke, lace, and imported materials.

What if the outfit doesn&apos;t fit perfectly?

We include up to two free alterations with every custom order. We conduct multiple fittings throughout the process to ensure a perfect fit before final delivery.

Do you make outfits for men?

Yes, we create custom pieces for both men and women. Our men&apos;s collection includes agbada, senator styles, suits, and traditional attire for all occasions.
